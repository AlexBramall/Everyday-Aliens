Who are the

Everyday Aliens?

Curious by nature, these extra-dimensional aliens had one goal, to explore. WShen their dimensional gateway experiment backfired, 10,000 of their greatest exploreres were unwittingly flung through space-time, bursting into the scene on Earth. Scattered and confused, they find themselves stranded, and having to adapt to the strange ways of humanity. They live amongst us, piecing together fragmented memories, hoping to find a way to contact home.
