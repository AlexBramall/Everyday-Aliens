Who are the

Everyday Aliens?

Curious by nature, these extra-dimensional aliens had one goal, to explore. When their dimensional gateway experiment backfired, 10,000 of their greatest exploreres were unwittingly flung through space-time, bursting into the scene on Earth. Scattered and confused, they find themselves stranded, and having to adapt to the strange ways of humanity. They live amongst us, piecing together fragmented memories, hoping to find a way to contact home.

Alien Rankings

An aliens' rank comes from its position on the Space Station, where they were preparing to explore the metaverse... that is before The Big Sploot happened. Now, with their memories wiped clean, all they have are their "Space Station Employment Badges" that hint at their past life. Higher ranking aliens seem to be regaining their memories at a faster rate... but why?
